Account & Data Deletion – Complete The Picture Game

App name: Complete The Picture Game
Developer: Doc Jana

How to request account and data deletion

To request deletion of your account and any associated data used by Complete The Picture Game, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to wordcaptions@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account & Data Deletion Request – Complete The Picture Game
  3. Include the Google account email used to sign in to the app

Requests are processed within 7–14 business days.

What data is deleted

  • Google Sign-In identifier associated with this app (if stored by us)
  • Any app-specific preferences or progress stored by us

Data that may be retained

This app uses Google services such as Google Play Services and AdMob. These services may collect and retain anonymized or aggregated data in accordance with Google’s privacy policies.

The app does not store passwords or authentication credentials.

Data retention period

  • App-specific user data: deleted within 14 days of request
  • Analytics or advertising data: retained as per Google’s policies
