Account & Data Deletion – Word Captions

App name: Word Captions
Developer: Doc Jana

How to request account and data deletion

To request deletion of your account and any associated data used by the Word Captions app, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to wordcaptions@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account & Data Deletion Request – Word Captions
  3. In the email, include the Google account email used to sign in to the app

Your request will be processed within 7–14 business days.

What data is deleted

Upon a valid deletion request, the following data (if applicable) will be deleted:

  • Google Sign-In identifier associated with the app
  • Any app-specific preferences or usage data stored by us

Data that may be retained

The app uses third-party Google services such as AdMob and Google Play Services. These services may collect and retain anonymized or aggregated data in accordance with Google’s privacy policies.

The app does not store passwords or authentication credentials.

Data retention period

  • App-specific user data: deleted within 14 days of request
  • Analytics or advertising data: retained as per Google’s policies
